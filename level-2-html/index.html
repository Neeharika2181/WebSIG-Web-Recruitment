DOGS

Dogs are loyal, intelligent, four-legged mammals, descendants of wolves, known as "Man's Best Friend" for their unconditional love, companionship, and service, acting as pets, guides, and protectors.

Key Characteristics & Behavior:
  • Loyalty & Affection: Dogs are famous for their unwavering devotion and ability to sense human emotions, offering comfort and companionship.
  • Intelligence & Senses: They possess a superior sense of smell and hearing, allowing them to detect high-pitched sounds and track scents for various tasks, notes National Geographic Kids.
  • Communication: They communicate through barks, tail wags (expressing joy), and body language, with yawning sometimes indicating anxiety, according to Pawsindia.
  • Physical Traits: With four legs, fur, and a tail, they come in countless breeds and sizes, with their skeletons adapted for running and flexibility.
